 When it comes to gold, it's pretty well known how and what it's used for

(jewelry, electrical components etc.). Of course these are the more commonly known uses.

The actually process of harvesting gold for commercial use is its own process,

involving a method of leaching gold using various solvents to harvest it. An example of

these methods is Gold cyanidation, a very common techinque used in the process of

extracting gold.
